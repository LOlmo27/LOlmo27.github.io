About Get To School 5:

Get To School is a smash hit indie success about getting to school...presumably. It takes a deep dive into classic RPG styles, while also keeping up with exciting new things! Get to School follows the main four characters Rinku, Uyru, Edward, and Levi on their adventures to school! They can explore, meet to charcaters, help their town, or build up their own home! Journey into the vast world of mazes and dungeons, explore new places, and fight powerful enemies, and meet new people! Off to adventure brave heroes!
